What has been changed?

This folder is a copy of the ThalamusDB and then an implementation of embedding the images and ordering the
images based on the embedding at the moment one (or more) certain rows are found. The the most
similar to the certain rows are sorted to come first. The only files that are changed are engine.py, 
relational.py and sementic_filter.py.
